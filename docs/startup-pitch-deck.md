# Startup Pitch Deck

## 1. Title

ResearchHub  
The easiest way for students to publish and showcase research, projects, and technical articles.

## 2. Problem

Millions of students complete research papers, capstone projects, final-year reports, and innovation ideas, but their work disappears into PDFs, college drives, and submission portals.

## 3. Solution

ResearchHub gives every student a free publication page, permanent link, public profile, PDF hosting, citation metadata, discovery, and analytics.

## 4. Why Now

Students increasingly need public proof of work for internships, placements, graduate school, and LinkedIn visibility. AI has also made research summarization and metadata generation easier.

## 5. Product

- Publish papers and project reports
- Generate abstracts, summaries, keywords, and citations
- Discover trending student research
- Build author profiles
- Track views and downloads
- Share permanent links on resumes and LinkedIn

## 6. Market

Initial wedge: engineering and university students in India and emerging markets.  
Expansion: universities, research clubs, hackathons, bootcamps, and global student communities.

## 7. Business Model

- AdSense on public pages
- Premium student memberships
- Featured publications
- Sponsored university pages
- Campus ambassador programs
- Institutional analytics

## 8. Go-To-Market

- LinkedIn launch loops
- University ambassadors
- Final-year project season campaigns
- SEO for project titles and categories
- Partnerships with colleges and technical clubs

## 9. Moat

- Student identity graph
- Publication SEO library
- University-level network effects
- Analytics and reputation layer
- Distribution through resumes and LinkedIn links

## 10. Ask

Raise pre-seed capital to build the student publishing graph across India, launch ambassador programs, and convert universities into sponsored partners.
