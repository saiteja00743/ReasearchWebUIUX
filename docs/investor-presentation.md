# Investor Presentation

## Executive Summary

ResearchHub is a free publishing and showcase platform for student research, final-year projects, technical articles, and innovation ideas. It turns dormant student work into public proof of work with SEO pages, analytics, AI metadata, and profile distribution.

## Core Insight

Students already produce valuable technical work, but the publishing system is fragmented, slow, and intimidating. ResearchHub makes publication feel as simple as uploading to LinkedIn, but with academic credibility.

## Product Loops

- Student publishes work
- Publication page is shared on LinkedIn and resumes
- Visitors discover related papers
- Authors gain followers and analytics
- Universities sponsor visibility

## Revenue

1. AdSense on high-intent public pages
2. Premium author profiles and analytics
3. Featured publication placements
4. Sponsored university pages
5. Institutional dashboards

## Key Metrics

- Published papers per week
- Activation rate from signup to first publication
- Publication views per author
- Download conversion rate
- Share-to-signup conversion
- Sponsored university pipeline

## 12-Month Milestones

- 100K registered students
- 250K published works
- 10M monthly page views
- 100 university/community partners
- First $25K MRR across premium and sponsorship products

## Risks

- Spam and low-quality uploads: mitigated by review queues, rate limits, and reputation scoring.
- Copyright issues: mitigated by takedown flow and author attestation.
- SEO dependence: mitigated by LinkedIn loops and university partnerships.
