# Product Launch Plan

## Launch Positioning

ResearchHub is where students publish research and project work for free, get a permanent link, and build public proof of work.

## Pre-Launch

- Recruit 50 student authors across 10 colleges
- Seed 250 high-quality publications
- Create category pages for AI, IoT, Data Science, Web, Social Impact
- Prepare LinkedIn launch assets
- Build ambassador waitlist
- Set up analytics dashboards

## Launch Week

- Day 1: Founder story and public beta launch
- Day 2: Showcase top 25 student publications
- Day 3: Campus ambassador announcement
- Day 4: Publish guide: how to put research on LinkedIn
- Day 5: University outreach campaign
- Day 6: Product Hunt or community launch
- Day 7: Metrics recap and student spotlight

## Post-Launch

- Weekly student research leaderboard
- Monthly university ranking
- SEO pages for top project topics
- Sponsored university pilot
- Premium analytics waitlist
