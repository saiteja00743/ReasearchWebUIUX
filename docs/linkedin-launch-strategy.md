# LinkedIn Launch Strategy

## Core Message

Students should not have to hide their best work in a PDF folder. ResearchHub gives every project, paper, and technical article a public home.

## Content Pillars

- Student proof of work
- Final-year project visibility
- Research publishing made simple
- Recruiter-friendly portfolios
- Founder building in public

## Launch Posts

1. Founder story: why ResearchHub exists
2. Demo video: publish a paper in 60 seconds
3. Student spotlight carousel
4. Before and after: PDF vs ResearchHub page
5. Call for campus ambassadors
6. University leaderboard
7. Milestone post after first 1,000 publications

## Student Sharing Loop

After publishing, prompt students to post:

```txt
I just published my project on ResearchHub:
[Title]
[Permanent link]

Open to feedback from students, faculty, and recruiters.
```

## Growth Hooks

- Auto-generate LinkedIn share text
- Add profile badge
- Weekly top research posts
- Comment templates for ambassadors
- Recruiter-facing collections
