# 30-Day Growth Strategy

## Goal

Reach 5,000 registered students, 2,000 published works, and 250,000 public page views in 30 days.

## Week 1: Seed Trust

- Publish 250 example papers
- Recruit 25 ambassadors
- Launch LinkedIn founder campaign
- Create SEO category pages
- Invite faculty reviewers

## Week 2: Student Acquisition

- Run "Publish Your Final-Year Project" campaign
- Partner with technical clubs
- Host 3 online publishing workshops
- Add LinkedIn sharing prompts
- Feature 50 student authors

## Week 3: Distribution Loops

- Launch weekly research leaderboard
- Create university pages
- Start referral rewards
- Publish recruiter-friendly collections
- Pitch student newsletters

## Week 4: Monetization Tests

- Test featured publication placements
- Open premium analytics waitlist
- Pitch sponsored university pages
- Add AdSense to high-traffic public pages
- Publish traction recap

## Metrics Dashboard

- Signup conversion
- First publication activation
- Upload completion rate
- Publication page views
- Download rate
- LinkedIn share rate
- Referral signups
- University partner leads
